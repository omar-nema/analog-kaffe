A little matcha stand with a slow heart.

Analog kaffe is a matcha pop-up run out of Hamilton Park. No coffee, no rush — just good matcha, whisked to order and poured over oat milk.

Everything starts with ceremonial-grade matcha and a bamboo whisk. We keep the menu short and the mornings unhurried. Whether it's a classic latte or a strawberry "mock"cha for the kids, it's made by hand while you wait. Find us in the park every other week — the exact dates live just below.
